# Counterfactual Scenario

This page describes a public counterfactual analysis: what scientific and technical capacity would be reduced if RegulonDB were unavailable or discontinued.

## Purpose

To document the expected impact on research workflows, reproducibility, and community efficiency in the absence of a curated regulatory reference for *Escherichia coli* K-12.

## Counterfactual Baseline

Without RegulonDB, users would need to reconstruct regulatory knowledge directly from dispersed literature and heterogeneous resources, with higher cost and lower consistency.

## Expected Consequences Without RegulonDB

## 1. Slower Scientific Discovery

- Longer time to assemble validated regulatory evidence.
- Reduced speed in generating and testing new hypotheses.
- Higher burden on individual labs for manual evidence integration.

## 2. Lower Reproducibility and Traceability

- Less consistent mapping between regulatory objects and supporting evidence.
- Increased variation in interpretation across studies.
- Reduced transparency in evidence provenance for downstream analyses.

## 3. Reduced Quality of Computational Inference

- Fewer high-confidence priors for network inference and systems modeling.
- Harder benchmarking of new algorithms and pipelines.
- Greater uncertainty in model validation against trusted curated references.

## 4. Fragmented Interoperability Across Resources

- Weaker linkage between regulatory knowledge and external databases.
- Higher integration cost for cross-platform workflows.
- Lower reuse efficiency in comparative and multi-resource studies.

## 5. Training and Capacity-Building Loss

- Fewer curated examples for teaching regulatory bioinformatics.
- Reduced access to structured, evidence-backed educational material.
- Higher entry barrier for students and early-career researchers.

## Public Value Preserved by RegulonDB

RegulonDB mitigates these risks by providing:

- Expert-curated, evidence-linked regulatory knowledge.
- Structured, reusable data objects and public documentation.
- Versioned releases and continuity-oriented governance practices.

## Methodological Note

- This counterfactual is a public narrative analysis based on observed usage patterns, published reuse examples, and known dependency of regulatory workflows on curated references.
- Quantitative indicators are reported in complementary public pages when available.
