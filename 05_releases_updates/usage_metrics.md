# Usage Metrics

This page reports public, aggregated web-usage indicators for RegulonDB based on Google Analytics 4 (GA4).

## Purpose

To provide transparent, reproducible, and non-sensitive usage indicators that support public reporting needs.

## Data Source and Method

- Source: Google Analytics 4 (GA4), traffic acquisition view.
- Scope: annual calendar windows.
- Metric level: aggregated public reporting only.
- Time windows used in this update:
  - 2024-01-01 to 2024-12-31
  - 2025-01-01 to 2025-12-31

## Annual Summary

| Year | Sessions | Engaged Sessions | Engagement Rate | Avg Engagement Time per Session | Events per Session |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 2024 | 32,662 | 17,624 | 53.9% | 1m 32s | 5.10 |
| 2025 | 58,259 | 24,834 | 42.63% | 56s | 4.61 |

## Channel Mix Snapshot

### 2024 (top channels by sessions)

- Organic Search: 19,794 (60.6%)
- Direct: 10,643 (32.59%)
- Referral: 2,023 (6.19%)

### 2025 (top channels by sessions)

- Direct: 32,003 (54.93%)
- Organic Search: 21,024 (36.09%)
- Referral: 3,967 (6.81%)

## Interpretation Notes

- Session volume increased from 2024 to 2025.
- Channel composition shifted from Organic Search-led traffic (2024) to Direct-led traffic (2025).
- Engagement rate and average engagement time changed between periods; interpretation should consider release cycles, campaign context, and user behavior mix.

## Public Reporting Limits

- This page intentionally reports aggregate metrics.
- User-level, device-level, and other potentially sensitive data are not published.
